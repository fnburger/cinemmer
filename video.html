Beyond the Decision

A three minute student short film depicting the process of decision making and internal struggle.

Tom, a risk-averse software developer, has a dream about his past where he faces a pivotal choice between taking a safe promotion at his corporate job or pursuing his uncertain start-up dream, only to realize that the true value lies not in immediate success but in taking the leap despite his fear of failure.
